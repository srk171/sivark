Textiles & Apparel

QTS offers a wide range of services to the Softlines industry at every stage of the supply chain. We support retailers, manufacturers, distributors and suppliers in testing textiles and apparel from the initial fabric and trim samples to the finished products. We ensure your products are compliant with all applicable US, EU, and international regulations in relation to physical, performance, and special claims testing. Some common testing criteria for fabrics, garments, household & miscellaneous textile items include:
  • Fiber Labeling
  • Care Labeling & Care Label Development
  • Colorfastness Evaluations
  • Dimensional Stability, Torque & Stretch & Recovery
  • Fabric Construction, Fiber Identification, Fabric Weight
  • Fabric, Garment and Seam Strength Tests
  • Compliance with US & EU Regulations
  • Performance like Abrasion, Pilling & so on

  • American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM)
  • Australian Standards (AS)
  • British Standards (BS)
  • Canadian General Standards Board (CGSB)
  • Canadian General Standards Board (CGSB)
  • European Standards (EN)
  • International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
  • Japanese Industrial Standards (JIS)
  • Guo Biao Standards (GB) issued by the Standardization Administration of China (SAC)
  • US Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) / Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act (CPSIA)
